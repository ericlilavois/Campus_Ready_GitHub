Welcome to Your Application

We're excited you're taking this important step toward your college journey

Campus Ready Grants help you begin college fully prepared. We provide travel assistance for you and a parent or guardian (including airfare, hotel and transportation), move-in essentials based on your college's checklist, and meals as you settle in.

Before You Begin

Along with basic contact information, you're going to need specific items to complete your application. Please have the following ready to make the process smooth and fast.

  • Your individual annual income (from jobs, wages, or tips)
  • Total household income and number of people in your household
  • FAFSA or California Dream Act Application (CADAA) completion status and your SAI (Student Aid Index), if completed
  • Name and address of the college you'll be attending
  • Expected start date and confirmation you'll be living on campus
